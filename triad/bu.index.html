How to beat King Elmer III with a deck of Minfilia, Sylph, Morbol, Bomb and Moogle.

Play your cards in the order of the blue numbers. The left side will show the current board and the right side will show possible future paths. Click on the path that King Elmer III has played.

If a card is blank, it means it doesn't matter which card King Elmer III plays in that slot (or another) just play your next card according to the next blue number.

If you play first, play Minfilia in the middle of the board and, after King Elmer III plays Godbert, click on the corresponding board to see next steps.

If King Elmer III plays first, click on the corresponding board on the right to see next steps.

If one of your cards is highlighted in red, no dice, it's impossible to win when you reach that point. Still follow instructions as they'll lead to a draw if possible (instead of a loss).
